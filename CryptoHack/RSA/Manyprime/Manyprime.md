### Solution

I used [factordb](http://factordb.com/) and saw that there was 32 primes.

I used a loop to calculate $\phi = (p1 - 1) * (p2 - 1) * ... * (p32 - 1)$

After having $\phi$, find d and decrypt the ciphertext like RSA Starter 5 to get the flag.
